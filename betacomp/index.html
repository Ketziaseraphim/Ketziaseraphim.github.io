About Beta Company

One of Australians major company that offers services such as civil infrastructure, open space and environmental services to both the public and private sector. The main objective of Beta is to expand their services across other states in Australia to become the leading Australian constructing service. The company is currently providing services such as environmental, engineering and maintenance to 30 different civil government and a few state government authorities in Victoria and New South Wales.
